Smash Burgers

Description

Smash burgers are a culinary delight that elevates the art of burger making to a delectable simplicity. These burgers distinguish themselves by their distinctive cooking method â€“ a ball of ground beef is expertly smashed onto a hot griddle, creating a thin, crispy, and caramelized exterior while locking in an irresistibly juicy interior. The result is a burger that boasts a perfect ratio of crispy edges to tender, flavorful meat. Typically cooked at a high heat to achieve the coveted crust, smash burgers are a celebration of minimalism in their ingredient list, often featuring just ground beef, salt, and pepper. This no-frills approach allows the natural flavors of the beef to shine, creating a mouthwatering experience that has made smash burgers a beloved icon in the world of casual, indulgent dining. Whether adorned with classic fixings or served simply with a slice of melty American cheese on a soft bun, the charm of the smash burger lies in its ability to deliver maximum flavor with uncomplicated perfection.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Condiments
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet
  2. Divide and shape patties
  3. Season the patties
  4. Smash and cook till brown
  5. Flip and add cheese
  6. Construct burger
  7. Serve
